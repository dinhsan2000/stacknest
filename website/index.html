Config Editor

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Stacknest free? ▼

Yes! Stacknest is completely free and open source. No hidden costs or limitations.

Which platforms are supported? ▼

Stacknest works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Download the installer for your platform.

Do I need to install services separately? ▼

Stacknest can manage system-installed services or use prebuilt binaries. Choose what works for you.

How do I report bugs or request features? ▼

Visit our GitHub repository at github.com/your-org/stacknest to report issues and contribute.

Can I use Stacknest in production? ▼

Stacknest is designed for local development. For production, use dedicated servers or cloud platforms.
